Awesome Features

Dashboard Report

Get the overall statistic of your business in the application dashboard. It will help merchants to measure their business status.

Merchant Management

Unlimited merchants can register in the application. Admin can adjust all the pricing for the merchants through the application.

Rider Management

Unlimited riders can be made and get assigned to the parcels to pickup and deliver to the destination.

Parcel Management

Parcel can be managed and created by merchants. Rider can change the status of parcel after picking up or on way to deliver it.

SMS Notification

Rider will get notified once he is assigned for a parcel to pickup. The customer of a merchant will get notified by a sms that the parcel is on the way to deliver.

Parcel Tracking

Merchant can easily track the parcel status and location of the created parcels by them.

Invoice & Others

Invoice can be generated for customers with all the pricing details is printed.
